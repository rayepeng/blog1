英语教材阅读总结

英语语法摘记

定语从句笔记

  • 先行词被形容词最高级修饰时，引导词必须用that
  • 先行词前面有序数词时，引导词必须用that
  • 先行词被only, no, one of, any, few, little, the same, the vary等词修饰时，引导词必须用that

  • 先行词本身就是all, something, everything, anything, nothing, none, the one等词时，引导词必须使用that

  • 非限定性定语从句时，必须使用which

  • 介词前置时，必须使用which
  • 先行词为地点名词，且在从句中作地点状语时，引导词使用where
  • 注意以下几种情况

it is the Suez which /that separates Asia from Africa

  • 在定语从句中，可以介词前置的引导词有whom，which

It is fitted with a small transformer, by means of which the voltage of the currency can be adjusted.

In a big family, children usually have more opportunities to talk to its different members, from whom they can obtain various experience and skills.

  • that 在指人的时候优先于whom，在指物的时候优先于which, 所以推荐使用that.

Mother is the first teacher that a child can have in his life.

  • whose的先行词可以是物也可以是人

  • 注意引导词why的用法

They totally understand the reason that/which I told them.

  • 注意引导词when的用法

I will never forget the time that/which we used to spend together.

The spring festival is held in the eve of the spring festival which/that is the most important day in china.

  • when的用法, was doing …when, be about to do… when
  • It is + 时刻 + when…
  • It has/is been + 段时间 + since…
  • It will be/was + 段时间 + before 过一段时间才
  • before 表示还未来得及就，或者过一段时间才
  • until的用法，not … until 和until
